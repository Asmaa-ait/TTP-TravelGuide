Travel Tips

Money & Currency

Currency exchange tips:

  • Exchange rates: When traveling certain country have different currency. So is IMPORTANT to check current rates before traveling.
  • Avoid exchanging from airport outlets
  • Watch for hidden costs
  • Research the exchange rate
  • Use local Cash
  • Check if your credit/debit card works internationally

Travel Documents

Suggested content:

  • Passport: Must be valid for at least 6 months beyond the travel date.

Visas:

  • Check if a visa is needed for your destination.
  • Apply in advance if required.

Photocopies: Carry digital and physical copies of your documents.

Pack Smart

Essentials cheklist:

  • Clothes appropriate to climate and culture
  • Toiletries(in travel sizes)
  • Chargers, adapters. Find the country adapter

Packing tips:

  • Roll clothes to save spaces
  • Use compression cubes for packing

Vaccinations

Suggested content:

  • Routine vaccines: Ensure Hepatitis A and B, flu, etc. are up to date.

COVID-19: Requirements vary by country.

Health kit: Includes basic meds(pain relievers, anti-diarrheal, band-aids).

Check CDC Travel for region-specific info.
